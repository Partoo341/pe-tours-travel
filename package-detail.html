Package Overview

Experience the beautiful Lake Nakuru National Park with this amazing package. See thousands of flamingos, rhinos, and other wildlife against the stunning backdrop of the Great Rift Valley. This package includes game drives, accommodation, and meals.

Highlights:

  • Game drive in Lake Nakuru National Park
  • See flamingos and rhinos
  • Comfortable accommodation
  • All meals included
  • Professional guide

Detailed Itinerary

1

Nairobi - Lake Nakuru

Depart Nairobi in the morning, drive through the Great Rift Valley. Arrive at Lake Nakuru National Park for lunch. Afternoon game drive to see flamingos, rhinos, and other wildlife. Dinner and overnight at the lodge.

2

Lake Nakuru - Nairobi

Early morning game drive. Breakfast at the lodge. Depart for Nairobi with lunch en route. Arrive Nairobi in the afternoon.
